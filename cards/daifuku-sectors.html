Sectors: Who This Model Applies To

The combined Phase 1 and Phase 2 framework — covering IoT monitoring, cloud-hosted shop management software, real-time production dashboards, carbon emissions accounting, work order tracking, and Cobot integration — is applicable to organizations in:

  • Manufacturing (automotive, aerospace, electronics, consumer goods): any facility with multi-cell or multi-line production and legacy equipment
  • Logistics and warehousing: facilities using automated or semi-automated material handling equipment
  • Food and beverage manufacturing: production scheduling and work order tracking across processing lines
  • Industrial maintenance operations: predictive and condition-based maintenance enabled by real-time data pipelines
  • Any SME seeking Industry 4.0 digital transformation with clear bottleneck-identification, energy management, or production tracking use cases
