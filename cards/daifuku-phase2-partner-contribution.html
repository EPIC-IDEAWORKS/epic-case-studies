Industry Partner Contribution — Phase 2

Daifuku Manufacturing Canada Corporation contributed the following to the Phase 2 collaboration:

  • Access to the Hamilton facility and its 20+ production cells for SMS integration, dashboard deployment, and Cobot programming
  • Production data, work order data, and scheduling context to inform dashboard and analytics pipeline design
  • Internal subject matter expertise on manufacturing workflows, bottlenecks, and reporting needs
  • Collaborative participation of the Daifuku Maintenance and Operations teams throughout development and deployment
  • Cobot hardware and associated operational environment for integration and testing
  • Plant-wide deployment of work-order document manager web app
