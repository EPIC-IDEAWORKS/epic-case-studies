Industry Partner Contribution — Phase 1

Daifuku Manufacturing Canada Corporation contributed the following to the Phase 1 collaboration:

  • Access to the Hamilton facility and all production cells for IoT sensor installation and system integration
  • Collaboration of the Daifuku Maintenance Team throughout design, installation, and commissioning
  • Operational data and production context to inform platform and dashboard design
  • Diverse equipment inventory, including legacy machinery dating back to the 1950s, as the integration environment for the IoT system
